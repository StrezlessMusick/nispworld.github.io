Title
Allied Disposition List (ADL)
Description

The Allied Disposition List (ADL) is an expression of the time-phased requirements for deploying the contributed forces. The ADL establishes the lines of communications, entry points, arrival sequence, timings, final destination and TOA for each element of the force package entering the theatre. It is based on the final destination and required delivery data established in the Statement of Requirements (SOR).
