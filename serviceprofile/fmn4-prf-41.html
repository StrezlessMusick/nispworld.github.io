Title
Formatted Messages for ISR Profile
Description
The Formatted Messages Profile for Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR) provides standard for formatted messages that are typically used to exchange ISR products in military operations. These formatted messages may be used as payload/attachment in combination with various transport mechanisms such as informal messaging (e-mail), text collaboration (chat) or for publication as files on websites. In addition, some of these formatted messages are also supported by federated ISR Libraries.
