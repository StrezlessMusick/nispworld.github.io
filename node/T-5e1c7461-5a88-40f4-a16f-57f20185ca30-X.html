Title
Course of Action (COA)
Description

A Course of Action (COA) is a description of a specific way of achieving a desired end state, be it for an entire mission or final conditions for a Fragmentary Order in getting the plan execution back in line with the OPLAN. The COA is dedicated to friendly forces and the ECOA (Enemy COA) is dedicated to hostile forces.
