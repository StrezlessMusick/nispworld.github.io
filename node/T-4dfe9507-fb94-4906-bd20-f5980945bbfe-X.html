Title
NATO Record
Description

A NATO Record is defined as information created, received and maintained as evidence and information by NATO, in pursuance of legal obligations, NATO missions or in the transaction of business. In short NATO records officially document the actions and decisions of the Organization. A NATO Record is therefore NATO information and as such is managed in accordance with the NATO Information Management Policy (NIMP) and related policies. However, the legal and evidential nature of records necessitates additional Policy to complement the NIMP and related policies. A systematic approach to the management of records is essential for NATO to protect and preserve records as evidence of actions and decisions, and to enable accountability. The NATO Records Policy (NRP) provides the basic principles and requirements for the management of NATO records.
