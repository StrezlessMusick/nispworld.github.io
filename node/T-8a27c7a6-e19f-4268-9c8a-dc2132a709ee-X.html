Title
NATO Emitter Database (NEDB)
Description

The purpose of the NATO Emitter DataBase (NEDB) is to provide for the NATO-wide exchange and distribution of parametric and other data on NATO and non-NATO non-communications electromagnetic emitters, in accordance with STANAG 6009. The NEDB supports the exchange of information relevant to EW on potential adversaries and provides a mechanism to make it available immediately to the SCs and operational commanders.
