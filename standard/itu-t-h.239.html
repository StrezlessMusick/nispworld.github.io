Title
Role management and additional media channels for H.300-series terminals

Applicability

Recommendation ITU-T H.239 defines the procedures for use of more than one video channel in ITU-T H.320-based systems, and for labelling individual channels with a role, which indicates requirements for processing the channel and the role of the channel content in the call. Role labels are applicable to both ITU-T H.320 and ITU-T H.245 signalling-based systems. The defined procedures include control, indication and capability exchange mechanisms.
