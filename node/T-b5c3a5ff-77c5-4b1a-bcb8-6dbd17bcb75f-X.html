Title
Mission Execution Environment Instance Provisioning Processes
Description

The Mission Execution Environment Instance Provisioning Processes describe the activities and tasks for the instantiation of a federated Mission Network. The instantiation of a federated Mission Network will occur in the context of a specific mission and will be established as part of the planning and execution of an operation.
