Title
Detailed Deployment Plan (DDP)
Description

The Detailed Deployment Plan describes the movement plan of (military) units. For OPLANs, Nations will utilize the ADL to develop their national DDPs considering Force Packaging, Bi/Multi-lateral Support Arrangements, Time Phasing, Strategic Lines of Communication (LOC), In-Theatre Modes of Transportation (MOT), Site Surveys, Strategic Modes of Transportation, and Assignment of Transportation Assets.
