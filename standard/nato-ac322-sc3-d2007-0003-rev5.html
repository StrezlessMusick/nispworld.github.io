Title
SMADEF XML Documentation Rel.3.0.0

Applicability

The objective of this document is to define the Concept of Operations (CONOPS) and data elements for the exchange of spectrum management related data, including but not limited to Locations, Spectrum Supportability and the associated Host Nation Replies, Allotments, temporary or permanent frequency Proposals and Assignments, Interference Reports, Joint Restricted Frequency Lists (JRFL), Communications Electronics Operating Instructions (CEOI), and Hazards of Electromagnetic Radiations to Ordinance (HERO). The CONOPS includes the purpose of Spectrum Management Allied Data Exchange Format - eXtensible Markup Language (SMADEF-XML), the business needs that SMADEF-XML satisfies, the basic concepts behind SMADEF-XML, and finally its characteristics and behaviour from the user point of view.
