Description

The Civil Logistic Capabilities are capabilites and their characteristics, either or not provided through Host Nation Support Authorities (HNSAs). They typically exist of a catalogue, list, directory, or standing data. Information about Civil Logistic Capabilities comes from * Country Books * Host Nation Catalogue * NSPA (Contract information, including BCIs, in their role as Contract Integrator) * National contingencies in the AOO
