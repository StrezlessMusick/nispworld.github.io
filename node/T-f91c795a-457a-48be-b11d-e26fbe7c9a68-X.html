Title
Merchant Warning (MERWARN)
Description

The Merchant Warning (MERWARN) is used as a simplified contamination warning system throughout NATO for broadcasting, via MERCOMMS and coastal radio stations, warnings of contamination dangerous to merchant shipping. This system calls for the origination, by NATO naval authorities, of five types of messages MERWARN NBC, MERWARN NBC 3 NUC, MERWARN NBC Chemical Downwind Message, MERWARN NBC 3 CHEM, MERWARN DIVERSION ORDER.
