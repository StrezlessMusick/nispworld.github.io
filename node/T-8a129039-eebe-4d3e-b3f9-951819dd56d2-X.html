Title
Joint Implementation Agreement (JIA)
Description

The Joint Implementation Agreement (JIA) obligates the signatories financially for the provision of resources. They detail costs and how support requirements will be provided to forces and are signed by the requesting nation (or NATO Commander) and the HN in question. JIAs are normally published as stand-alone documents but the information they contain may sometimes be included in a Technical Arrangement (TA).
