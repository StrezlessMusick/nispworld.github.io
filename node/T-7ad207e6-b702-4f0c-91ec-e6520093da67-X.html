Title
JFACC Mission Statement
Description

The JFACC Mission Statement is the output of the Mission Analysis. It is formulated to provide a clear, concise statement detailing who will conduct the operation, what is to be done, when it will take place, where it will occur, and why it is being conducted (e.g. the purpose of the operation). However, the Mission Statement does not state how the operation will be conducted. It is reviewed to ensure that it identifies the command’s mission-essential tasks required to achieve the higher authority’s objective and desired end-state.
