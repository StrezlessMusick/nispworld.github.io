Title
Synchronisation Matrix (Sync Matrix)
Description

For each COA the key actions of subordinates should be synchronised in time and space according to the concept of the operation. This process provides a further opportunity for the staff to evaluate the feasibility of each COA. Synchronisation can be evaluated during war gaming or as a separate step, but should be completed as a part of the analysis process. (See the example Synchronisation Matrix at Annex H).
