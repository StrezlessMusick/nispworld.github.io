Title
Logistics Collaborative Planning Processes
Description

The Logistics Planning processes are integral part of and support the OPP processes. They are initiated as part of the Operations Planning Process at the respective level (strategic/ operational/ component) in support to a particular planning phase. The Level 2 Logistics Collaborative Planning processes are grouped into three categories based on the level at which they are executed. Furthermore, one or more Logistics Planning processes at a given level will map to (be executed as part of) a certain phase of the OPP.
