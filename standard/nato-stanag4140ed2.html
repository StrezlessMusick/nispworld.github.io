Title
Adoption of a Standard Target Acquisition Meteorological Message

Applicability

The aim of this STANAG is to define a target acquisition meteorological message for remotely-piloted vehicle, drone, weapon locating radar and sound ranging systems, and to standardise the number of its information digits and their meanings for the use by any service of any country. This will make it possible for all armed forces to

  • Produce the message either by conventional or automatic data processing (ADP) methods;
  • Understand and use the message with either conventional or ADP based drone, weapon location radar and sound ranging systems.
