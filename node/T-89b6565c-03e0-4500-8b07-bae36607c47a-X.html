Title
Medical Common Operational Picture (MEDCOP)
Description

Medical Treatment Facilities (MTFs) and MEDEVAC Asset Stations (like HLS) are to update the MEDCOP tool with their status information as soon as possible, but at least every 6-hours; for MTFs this includes details on beds established and utilized in summary format, for all this includes traffic-light indicators on overall assessment of mission readiness, asset/capability availability and wheater situation.
