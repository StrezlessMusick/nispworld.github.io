Title
OPP Courses of Action Decision Process
Description

The Operational Planning Processes (OPP) Courses of Action (COA) Decision Process supports the decision making of a Joint Force Command (JFC) on a chosen COA, and then refine this COA as the future core of the Concept of Operations (CONOPS). The prerequisites for this process are a set of refined COAs; the staff recommended COA; the COA selection rationale; JFC’s personal analysis having earlier led to the COA selection criteria in earlier processes. The desired outputs are JFC’s COA selection; COA modifications; a refined JFC’s intent. These will then be inputs to the CONOPS development in the subsequent step.
