Title
Blue Order of Battle (ORBAT)
Description

The Blue Order Of Battle (ORBAT) is used to inform major commanders in peacetime and in periods of crisis and war of changes in the order of battle for coalition forces and thereby to assure that the most current information is available for operational planning.
