Title
Mine Counter Measures Operations Directives (MCMOPDIR)
Description

Mine Counter Measures OPerations DIRectives. Is a formal message detailing the following - Assigned operating areas - Risk Directives Matrix, detailing allowable risks for all risk directives mentioned in MCMOPORD, the default matrix is received from the relevant Nations - Identification of Standing routes and channels, QZR numbers (i.e. references to chart numbers, with charts on optical disks, provided by NMW Data Center at Taunton, UK) - Courses of action, i.e. route priorities, based on commander’s intent & allowable risks (from MCMOPORD), MTA’s and other intelligence relevant to the mission, order of magnitude calculations (e.g. How long does it take to clear one mile?) - Free text on commander’s intent. The MCMOPDIR is issued by Strategic-level MCM Commander, but usually completed by Operational-level MCM.
