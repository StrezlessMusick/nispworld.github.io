JFAC Warning Order
Description

The JFAC Warning Order is used to provide initial guidance to JFAC staff, the Air Operations Planning Group and subordinate units to enable the initiation of the Air Operations Planning Process (A-OPP). The document template may be found in the Functional Planning Guide for Joint Air Operations Planning (reference) Thi stempalte is derived from the Operational Warning Order template located in the Comprehensive Operations Planning Directive (COPD).
