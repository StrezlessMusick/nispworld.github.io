Title
Operational Advice on Military Response Options
Description

Operational Advice on Military Response Options is a document providing advice and recommendations to SACEUER on draft Military Response Options (MRO), with details on each MRO provided in attached annexes. The document contains a succinct summary of the main conclusions and operational concerns/advice from the analysis and comparison of the different MROs as to their adequacy, merits and potential for operational success. The document template may be found in the annexes to the Comprehensive Operations Planning Directive (reference).
