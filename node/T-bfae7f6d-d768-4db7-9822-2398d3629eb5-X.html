Title
Operations Assessment Plan
Description

The Operations Assessment Plan (OAP) is used to evaluate operations planning and execution. The plan identifies multiple sources, collection strategies and procedures for gathering and exploiting enemy intelligence and friendly information derived from sources such as ISR, operations, logistics, communications and others. The plan also directs process owners to record and provide required data to reports cells adn/or operations assessors. The OAP may be published as an appendix to OPLANs, subordinate or supplemental plans.
