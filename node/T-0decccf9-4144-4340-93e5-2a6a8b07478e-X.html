Title
Retention & Disposition Matrix
Description

Better Information Management Retention & Disposition will improve information handover and the retention of information for reference and legal purposes. The Matrix represents a structured way to control NATO Information determining its long-term value and the subsequent actions (archiving or destruction) when the information is no longer needed for the conduct of the current business.
