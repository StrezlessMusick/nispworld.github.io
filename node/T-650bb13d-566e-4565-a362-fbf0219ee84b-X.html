Title
Conceptual Framework for Allied Operations (CFAO)
Description

Summarised in a single document for coherence and clarity of purpose, the Bi-SC Conceptual Framework for Allied Operations (CFAO) (a) Describes how Alliance forces will be commanded, structured and employed on operations; (b) Establishes the NATO Deployable Forces (NDF) concept applicable to NCS and NFS; (c) Provides D&G for the development of ACO Force Standards (AFS) volumes with linkage to ACO Directives, SAGE and other supporting documents.
