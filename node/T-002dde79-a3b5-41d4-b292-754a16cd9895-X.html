Title
Staff Estimate
Description

A Staff Estimate is the product of a functional analysis conducted during the Operations Planning Process that contributes to the development of Concepts of Operations (CONOPS) and Operations Plans (OPLAN). The staff estimate includes assessment of a situation that includes evaluation of how factors in staff selectionâ€™s functional area (logistics, personnel, CIS, etc) can influence each Course of Action (COA). It also provides information critical for COA selection to determine which COA best accomplishes the mission and is most feasible. Once a COA is selected, refined staff estimates provide the basis for annexes to OPLANs, including commander, operations, personnel, inteligence, logistics, communications and special staff estimates.
