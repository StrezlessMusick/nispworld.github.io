Title
Battle Damage Assessment (BDA)
Description

The Battle Damage Assessment (BDA) is a report containing the timely and accurate estimate of damage resulting from the application of military force, either lethal or non-lethal, against a predetermined objective (US Jt Pub 1-02). The assessment occurs in three phases 1. Phase 1 BDA physical damage 2. Phase 2 BDA functional damage (Operational damage) 3. Phase 3 BDA overall picture of the status of the enemy capability and progress of the campaign.
