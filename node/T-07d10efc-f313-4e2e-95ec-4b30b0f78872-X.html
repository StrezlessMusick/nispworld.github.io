Title
Strategic Environmental Analysis
Description

The RDG reviews and updates the analysis of the strategic environment (PMESII, meteorological and oceanographic (METOC) and geospatial) developed during Phase 1, while identifying any critical gaps in information and knowledge. This analysis will be developed with an integrated gender perspective and should consider Children and Armed Conflict (CAAC) issues. The aim is to identify those key issues, factors and actors influencing the crisis; and its possible causes and possible resolution.
