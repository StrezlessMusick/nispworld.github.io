Title
Federated Situational Awareness Processes
Description

Federated Situational Awareness provides the group of processes supporting federated SA and Common Operational Picture (COP) Management required for the successful end-to-end execution of SA/COP processes in a coalition environment. The processes describe generic COP management and a tailorable compendium of processes for management of Functional and Operational Domain Pictures.
