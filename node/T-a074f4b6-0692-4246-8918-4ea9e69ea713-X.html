Title
Airspace Control Means (ACM)
Description

The Airspace Control Means (ACM) are procedural measures that when established, reserve airspace for specific airspace users, restrict the action of airspace users, control actions of specific airspace users, and/or require airspace users to accomplish specific actions. ACMs can also be used to identify friendly users, provide separation from other friendly airspace users and avoid the risk of being engaged by friendly air defence weapons.
