Title
Theatre Movements Architecture
Description

The Theatre Movements Architecture contains the principal aspects of logistic movement in theatre. This architecture includes APOD/SPODs and other key transportation nodes such as railheads; Staging areas and facilities required for operational entry into the JOA; Reception areas and facilities; and Lines of Communication (LOC) to and within areas of operation.
