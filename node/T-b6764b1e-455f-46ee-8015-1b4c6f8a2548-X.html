Title
Intelligence Collection Plan (ICP)
Description

The Intelligence Collection Plan (ICP) is a detailed breakdown of how each intelligence requirement (IR) is to be satisfied. It will indicate the general level of detail required and will list the organisations, agencies or assets best suited to the task. The ICP is developed as part of the Intelligence Cycle.
