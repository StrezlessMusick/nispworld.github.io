Maritime Person of Interest List
Description

Maritime persons of Interest include owners, past and potential crew members or pirates. The related maritime interests person shall be defined with information like identity, kind of relation with the maritime interests (owner, crew member, pirate), activity, skills, history of the commitment in the maritime community (former crews)
