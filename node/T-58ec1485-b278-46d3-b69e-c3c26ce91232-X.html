Title
Military Response Options (MRO)
Description

Military Response Options (MRO) constitute possible uses of the NATO military instrument to assist with crisis resolution. An MRO outlines a military mission, Military Strategic Objectives (MSO), Military Strategic Effects (MSE), Military Strategic Actions (MSA) and capability requirements, as well as possible Complementary Non-Military Actions (CNMAs). MROs should be clearly distinguishable from one another; they must be unambiguous and sufficiently detailed to provide decision makers with clear and realistic options in response to a crisis.
